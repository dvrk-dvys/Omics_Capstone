Title	Identification of Potential Biomarkers for Improving the Precision of Early Detection of Steroid-induced Osteonecrosis of the Femoral Head
Organism	Homo sapiens
Experiment type	Expression profiling by array
Summary	Early diagnosis and prompt treatment are the key to prevent collapse and delay Steroid-induced Osteonecrosis of the Femoral Head(SONFH) progression.The aim of this study was to identify potential biomarkers for the diagnosis of SONFH, so to improve the efficiency of early diagnosis.
Microarray analysis based on the 30 SONFH patients and 10 non-SONFH patients(following steroid administration) was performed to screen SONFH-related genes.

Overall design	The aim of this study was to identify potential biomarkers for the diagnosis of SONFH. The gene expression profile was detected by microarray analysis based on the 30 SONFH patients and 10 non-SONFH patients. Then, a list of candidate gene biomarkers of SONFH were identified by integrating differential expression data analysis and gene signal transduction network analysis.
